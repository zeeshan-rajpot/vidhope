About Videhope

About videhope Our company Videhope LLC is a company registered in the United States of America and built specifically to help africa creators to monetize their content and give the possibilities to their fans to support them in their local currency. The app is easy to use and available to everyone and everywhere. Our mission Videhope has a purpose to become the biggest social media and subscription service that helps African creators to have deep connections with their fans. The app is inclusive of artists and content creators from all genres and allows them to monetize their content while developing authentic relationships with their super fans.
